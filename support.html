Frequently asked

How do drivers sign in?
Your fleet admin creates accounts and invites drivers. Drivers sign in with their email and start inspections right away.

Does it work without signal?
Yes. Inspections are saved on the device and submit automatically once the phone is back online.

What inspections are supported?
Guided pre-trip and post-trip driver vehicle inspections (DVIR), zone by zone, with photo capture for safety-critical items.

Who can review inspections?
Safety managers and admins review and approve inspections; a DVIR PDF is generated for each one.
